2. Variational Perspective: From VAEs to DDPMs

2.1 Variational Autoencoder

Model structure

x→encoder,qθ(z∣x)z∼N(0,1)→decoder,pϕ(x∣z)x^x\xrightarrow{\mathrm{encoder},q_{\theta}(z|x)} z\sim \mathcal{N}(0,1) \xrightarrow{\mathrm{decoder}, p_{\phi}(x|z)} \hat{x} xencoder,qθ​(z∣x)​z∼N(0,1)decoder,pϕ​(x∣z)​x^

Key formulae:

The learned data distribution:

pϕ(x)=Ep(z)[pϕ(x∣z)]=∫pϕ(x∣z)p(z)dzp_{\phi}(x) = \mathbb{E}_{p(z)}[p_{\phi}(x|z)] = \int p_{\phi}(x|z)p(z)dz pϕ​(x)=Ep(z)​[pϕ​(x∣z)]=∫pϕ​(x∣z)p(z)dz

Intractable to compute due to integral over latent variable zzz.

Maximizing log-likelihood   ⟺  \iff⟺ minimizing the KL divergence between true data distribution q(x)q(x)q(x) and learned data distribution pϕ(x)p_{\phi}(x)pϕ​(x):

arg⁡max⁡ϕEq(x)[log⁡pϕ(x)]  ⟺  arg⁡min⁡ϕEq(x)[log⁡q(x)−log⁡pϕ(x)]  ⟺  arg⁡min⁡ϕKL(q(x)∣∣pϕ(x))\begin{aligned} \arg\max_{\phi} \mathbb{E}_{q(x)}[\log p_{\phi}(x)] &\iff \arg\min_{\phi} \mathbb{E}_{q(x)}[\log q(x) - \log p_{\phi}(x)] \\ &\iff \arg\min_{\phi} \mathrm{KL}(q(x) || p_{\phi}(x)) \end{aligned} argϕmax​Eq(x)​[logpϕ​(x)]​⟺argϕmin​Eq(x)​[logq(x)−logpϕ​(x)]⟺argϕmin​KL(q(x)∣∣pϕ​(x))​

Evidence Lower Bound (ELBO):

Idea:

We try to estimate pϕ(x)p_{\phi}(x)pϕ​(x) by Bayes’ theorem

pϕ(x)=pϕ(x,z)pϕ(z∣x)p_{\phi}(x) = \frac{p_{\phi}(x,z)}{p_{\phi}(z|x)} pϕ​(x)=pϕ​(z∣x)pϕ​(x,z)​

Now the posterior pϕ(z∣x)p_{\phi}(z|x)pϕ​(z∣x) is also intractable, so we introduce a variational distribution qθ(z∣x)q_{\theta}(z|x)qθ​(z∣x) to approximate it.

Lower bound of the log-likelihood log⁡pϕ(x)\log p_{\phi}(x)logpϕ​(x):

log⁡pϕ(x)=Eqθ(z∣x)[log⁡pϕ(x,z)pϕ(z∣x)]=Eqθ(z∣x)[logpϕ(x,z)qθ(z∣x)⋅qθ(z∣x)pϕ(z∣x)]=Eqθ(z∣x)[log⁡pϕ(x,z)qθ(z∣x)]+Eqθ(z∣x)[log⁡qθ(z∣x)pϕ(z∣x)]=Eqθ(z∣x)[log⁡pϕ(x,z)qθ(z∣x)]+KL(qθ(z∣x)∣∣pϕ(z∣x))≥Eqθ(z∣x)[log⁡pϕ(x,z)qθ(z∣x)]=L(θ,ϕ;x)\begin{aligned} \log p_{\phi}(x) &= \mathbb{E}_{q_{\theta}(z|x)}\left[\log \frac{p_{\phi}(x,z)}{p_{\phi}(z|x)}\right] \\ &= \mathbb{E}_{q_{\theta}(z|x)}\left[\\log \frac{p_{\phi}(x,z)}{q_{\theta}(z|x)} \cdot \frac{q_{\theta}(z|x)}{p_{\phi}(z|x)}\right] \\ &= \mathbb{E}_{q_{\theta}(z|x)}\left[\log \frac{p_{\phi}(x,z)}{q_{\theta}(z|x)}\right] + \mathbb{E}_{q_{\theta}(z|x)}\left[\log \frac{q_{\theta}(z|x)}{p_{\phi}(z|x)}\right] \\ &= \mathbb{E}_{q_{\theta}(z|x)}\left[\log \frac{p_{\phi}(x,z)}{q_{\theta}(z|x)}\right] + \mathrm{KL}(q_{\theta}(z|x) || p_{\phi}(z|x)) \\ &\geq \mathbb{E}_{q_{\theta}(z|x)}\left[\log \frac{p_{\phi}(x,z)}{q_{\theta}(z|x)}\right] \\ &= \mathcal{L}(\theta, \phi; x) \end{aligned} logpϕ​(x)​=Eqθ​(z∣x)​[logpϕ​(z∣x)pϕ​(x,z)​]=Eqθ​(z∣x)​[logqθ​(z∣x)pϕ​(x,z)​⋅pϕ​(z∣x)qθ​(z∣x)​]=Eqθ​(z∣x)​[logqθ​(z∣x)pϕ​(x,z)​]+Eqθ​(z∣x)​[logpϕ​(z∣x)qθ​(z∣x)​]=Eqθ​(z∣x)​[logqθ​(z∣x)pϕ​(x,z)​]+KL(qθ​(z∣x)∣∣pϕ​(z∣x))≥Eqθ​(z∣x)​[logqθ​(z∣x)pϕ​(x,z)​]=L(θ,ϕ;x)​

Short summary:

By introducing a latent variable zzz and an inaccurate posterior approximation qθ(z∣x)q_{\theta}(z|x)qθ​(z∣x), we derive a lower bound of the log-likelihood log⁡pϕ(x)\log p_{\phi}(x)logpϕ​(x), which is called Evidence Lower Bound (ELBO). It is the expectation of the Bayes-like ratio pϕ(x,z)qθ(z∣x)\frac{p_{\phi}(x,z)}{q_{\theta}(z|x)}qθ​(z∣x)pϕ​(x,z)​ under the qθ(z∣x)q_{\theta}(z|x)qθ​(z∣x).
