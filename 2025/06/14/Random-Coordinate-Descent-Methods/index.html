Optimal Solution Set:
$E = {y \in Y : g(y) = \min_{z \in Y} g(z)}$

Alternative Characterization of $E$:
$E = {y \in Y : d(y, Q) = d(Y, Q)}$

where $d(y, Q) = \min{z \in Q} |y - z|$ and $d(Y, Q) = \min{y \in Y} d(y, Q)$.
